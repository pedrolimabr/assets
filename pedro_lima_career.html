Pedro
Lima

Born April 19, 2010 in São Paulo, Pedro Alves de Lima began karting at age 12. By 15, he had become the youngest driver ever to win a race in BRB Fórmula 4 Brasil — taking pole, leading every lap, and crossing the line just 0.053s clear of the field at Velocitta.

He closed his rookie F4 Brazil season with 11 podiums across 21 races and finished 3rd overall in the championship. In 2026 he signed with Dutch powerhouse Van Amersfoort Racing to begin his European campaign, carrying car #53 through the F4 Winter Series, Italian F4 and EuroF4.

4Brazil

2025 🇧🇷

Pedro's rookie season in Brazil's FIA-sanctioned junior single-seater category. The youngest driver on the grid finished 3rd in the championship, claimed two race wins, his first pole position, and 11 podiums across 21 starts — racing for Starrett Bassani F4.

⭐ Historic Milestone — Velocitta, Round 3

Starting from pole position, Pedro led every lap of Race 3 and held off reigning championship leader Heitor Dall'Agnol to the flag, winning by just 0.053 seconds — also picking up the point for fastest lap. The result made him the youngest driver ever to win a BRB Fórmula 4 Brasil race.
